Meet The Hackerati

Pushing forward. Giving back.

The Hackerati will solve some of the most challenging problems facing your business today: developing world-class technologies, recruiting top engineering talent, making great engineers even better, and providing an environment for ongoing innovation.

We're problem solvers.

Engineering as a Service

The Hackerati provides engineering services with no long-term commitments, allowing your business to rapidly pivot within your budget and timeframe. You get full visibility and control, you only pay for what you use, and you don't need to worry about recruiting and retaining elusive talent. We work 100% on-shore—and often on-site—to maximize velocity and agility.

We're teachers & students.

The Hackerati Academy

Technology is constantly changing, so we must constantly be learning. It all starts at The Hackerati Academy—our engineering training ground—where we build a foundation of good habits and best practices. We’re continuously adding advanced topics like Scala, Node.js, Hadoop, Mahout, iOS, and Android. Our team includes a blend of seasoned practitioners and instructors from leading-university engineering programs.

We're innovators.

The Hackerati Labs

We love to build cool stuff. It's in our DNA. So we created The Hackerati Labs, an R&D incubator that encourages all Hackerati to develop their own product ideas. They can pitch their ideas to our internal investment board for funding and resources, and even leverage our network of outside investors to form independent companies.

Engineering as a Service

Recruiting and retaining top technical talent is a challenge. Through Engineering as a Service, The Hackerati offers you fast cycle times, increased agility, and efficient use of capital compared to hiring and retaining engineers.

We field domestic engineering teams that are often based locally, and can even work on-site with the rest of your team. Our guild model blends teams of masters, experienced engineers, and student apprentices, providing us with the flexibility to work on your projects independently, collaborate with your existing team, or simply become your engineering department. And you’ll only ever pay for the engineering capacity that you use.

Whether you need to build a new product, accelerate a key development effort, leverage a new technology, or advance a long-term strategic initiative, we’ll help realize your goals without sacrificing agility, quality, time, or control over outcomes.

Building A New Product?

Need help building a great idea into a new product? The Hackerati’s extensive experience in bringing products to market will substantially reduce the risk of your development initiative.

Since you’ll only be paying for the engineering that you use, we’ll optimize your use of capital by rapidly delivering your prototype or minimum viable product. Need to pause development to secure more funding? No problem! Need to pivot? Easy! By functioning just like your own full-time employees, The Hackerati gives you full visibility and control over outcomes and complete ownership of all intellectual property and work products.

Need To Move Faster?

Have a great engineering team but need to expand it to accomplish your objectives? We understand that hiring engineers is difficult and that competition for top talent is fierce.

With Engineering as a Service, you can get to work right away without struggling through prolonged recruiting efforts or agonizing vendor negotiations. We can turn up a top team on very short notice, and can just as easily turn down your team or substitute team members as your situation changes. We’ll blend into your established methodologies or help you institute our best practices for agile development—whichever you prefer.

Want The Latest Tech?

Need a mobile or social app, big data solution, analytics, natural language processing, machine learning, or custom hardware?

As experts in emerging technology, The Hackerati will bring you as close to the bleeding edge as your business needs while protecting you from risk. We combine product experts, researchers, and thought leaders with seasoned project managers, architects, and top engineers to bring your world-class products to market.

Going Long?

Whether opening new markets, testing new business models, or developing new technologies, strategic initiatives are essential to the long-term success of your business. But focusing on long-term initiatives is often hard to balance against short-term tactical demands.

We’ll help you keep the lights on while your internal team focuses on developing your next-generation technology. Or we can deliver your next generation product while your internal team focuses on immediate deliverables. It’s your call.

How We Work

The Hackerati will work with your team continuously throughout your projects to ensure that we’re delivering the right solutions for your business, even as you adapt to constantly changing conditions.

We work in sprints that typically range from one week to one month. Sprints are dictated by collaborative planning sessions to determine your success criteria, and collaborative retrospectives to measure our progress. We provide complete transparency in our work, giving you full control over costs and outcomes. No surprises. Whether working in an existing code base or building a new one, we deliver releasable, production-grade code with every sprint. We practice unit testing, continuous integration, peer code reviews, and architecture reviews to minimize risk and maximize project velocity and agility.

We’re only as successful as you are, so we'll do everything possible to ensure your success.
