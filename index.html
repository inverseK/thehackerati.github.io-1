Huffington Post

Big Data Analytics

HuffPost also called upon The Hackerati to take over the management and development of their Big Data systems when they began transitioning their analytics team. We documented, updated and refactored these systems to align them with new performance and service-level expectations. We then trained their new team in the operation, development,and deployment of these systems.

The transitioned projects were two analytics systems—both realtime and batch-processed—collecting over 330 terabytes of new data every month and processing 10,000 events per second. We also worked on a machine learning/natural language processing system used to classify user-generated content, and a backend community system that handled over 300 million user comments.
