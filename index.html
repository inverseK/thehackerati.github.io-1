Big Data

In 2013 The Hackerati led the management and development of big data systems for The Huffington Post during an internal transition.

Our team updated and refactored these systems to bring them in line with new performance and service-level expectations, and trained the new Huffington Post team on the the operation, development, and deployment of these systems.

The projects transitioned were two analytics systems, both real-time and batch-processed, with over 330TB of new data every month, and also a machine learning and NLP system for the classification of user generated content.
