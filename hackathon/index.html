HackathOn The Mountain

Apply Sponsor

24 Hours | May 2015 | Metzgar's Farm

An open-air sustainability hackathon atop a mountain in Northeastern Pennsylvania.

Why Sustainability?

Humans have done enough damage to this planet to ensure our eventual destruction.
Our only chance at survival is to innovate and evolve our outlook.
There may be technologies that we haven't yet discovered.
The purpose of this event is to provide aid to future generations.

Our only hope is to create.
Our current trajectory suggests an expiration date.
Technological innovation is our only hope.
There's no time to waste!

Why Outdoors?

Reintegrating the NYC tech scene into the natural world will communicate the importance of sustainability far better than words.

The ability to directly interact with the environment will inspire the creative process.

  • build a tree-planting app while sitting in the forest
  • hack solar cells while basking in the sun
  • address pollution politics while breathing clean mountain air

Why Participate?

  • You care about the environment thriving.
  • You care about our species not dying.
  • You care about hacking cool hardware and software.
  • You don't care at all, but want to look like you're trying.

Whatever your motivation to join us atop the Mountain, we hope that you do. Everyone who gets involved - either by hacking or sponsoring [or both] - will be contributing to the promotion and optimization of a sustainable civilization.
