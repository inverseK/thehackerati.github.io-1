Hack·er

noun  \'ha-kər\

a creator who thrives on the intellectual challenge of solving problems by overcoming limitations

see also:

The Hack·er·a·ti

plural noun  \'ha-kər-är-tē\

a fearless engineering team delivering software and hardware development onshore—and often onsite—to maximize velocity and agility

Check out our blog post on the Hackerati's culture of innovation!

Meet The Hackerati

Pushing forward. Giving back.

Engineering as a Service

Our Agile consulting model that provides clients with top engineering talent at competitive rates.

Hackerati Academy

A platform for the ongoing education of Hackerati, prospective hires, and, eventually, the general public.

Hackerati Labs

An accelerator where Hackerati can get funding to develop and commercialize their startup ideas.
