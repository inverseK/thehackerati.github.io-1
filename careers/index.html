The Guild Model

The Hackerati appreciates that the best way to learn new skills is through guided, hands-on experience, as demonstrated by medieval guilds. Just as master artisans passed on secrets of the trade to less experienced students, The Hackerati cultivates an environment of continuous education tailored to producing master engineers.

  • Apprentice = Student Intern
  • Journeyman = Experienced Engineer
  • Master = Industry Veteran
