The Guild Model

At The Hackerati, we believe that the best way to learn new skills is through guided, hands-on experience. So we've taken a page from the playbook of medieval guilds–in which master artisans passed on the secrets of their trade to those who were less experienced–to create an environment of continuous teaching and learning, tailored to producing master engineers.
